Letter From The President

Dear Delegates,

It is my immense pleasure to welcome you to ŞEOMUN'26. As President, I am thrilled to see passionate young minds come together to engage in diplomacy, debate, and collaborative problem-solving. Our conference aims to foster understanding, inspire leadership, and create a space where innovative solutions to global challenges can be discussed.

The responsibility of guiding this event and working alongside our dedicated secretariat has been incredibly rewarding. We have meticulously prepared workshops, committee simulations, and interactive activities to ensure that your participation is both educational and memorable. We believe that every voice has value, and every delegate can contribute to shaping the discussions in meaningful ways.

As President, I encourage you to actively participate, challenge assumptions respectfully, and cultivate connections with delegates from diverse backgrounds. Through dialogue, negotiation, and critical thinking, we hope you will experience the transformative power of diplomacy.

Together, we uphold the tradition of excellence, nurture our community, and celebrate the diverse perspectives that each delegate brings. I hope your experience at ŞEOMUN'26 will be transformative, challenging, and unforgettable.

Best wishes,
Yusuf İslam İlkis
President

Letter From The Secretaries-General

Dear Delegates,

It is with immense pride and great enthusiasm that we invite you all to the seventh edition of ŞEOMUN'26, which will be held this July at our historic campus.

Our names are Defne Yıldız and Eren Ertaş, and we have had the honor of being part of ŞEOMUN for several years. In the past, we have served in various roles, including Academic Assistants and Under-Secretary-Generals, and now we are proud to serve as your Secretaries-General. Being part of this conference has been transformative for us and it is a privilege to carry forward the legacy of those before us.

ŞEOMUN has always been more than just a conference; it is a community, a platform, and a tradition of excellence that inspires growth, understanding, and connection. Every year, it gathers the brightest minds to discuss pressing global issues. Our diverse committees will challenge delegates to develop meaningful solutions, debate passionately, and forge lifelong connections.

In this year's sessions, you will encounter complex global issues, engage in thorough research, and collaborate to draft resolutions that could serve as templates for real-world diplomacy. The skills you acquire—public speaking, negotiation, critical thinking—will extend far beyond this conference, shaping your development as a global citizen and future leader.

On behalf of the entire Secretariat, we extend our warmest welcome to ŞEOMUN'26. Let us come together to honor this tradition, build on its legacy, and create yet another unforgettable chapter in its story.

Best wishes,
Defne Yıldız & Eren Ertaş
Secretaries-General
